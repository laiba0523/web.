FITNESS

Fitness is the foundation of a healthy and active lifestyle. It is not just about workouts or strict diets, but maintaining a good lifestyle that keeps the body and mind in shape. Health and fitness mean the complete presence of physical, mental, and social well-being. One can maintain their health and fitness in many ways, but it requires patience, hard work, and commitment towards a good lifestyle.

WHAT DOES IT MEAN TO BE FIT!

According to the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans set forth by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), there are five components of physical fitness:

Cardiorespiratory Fitness
Your VO2 max is a commonly used measure of this. It’s your body’s ability to uptake and utilize oxygen, which feeds all of your tissues. This is directly related to your health and quality of life.
Musculoskeletal Fitness
This includes muscle strength, endurance, and power.
Flexibility
This refers to the range of motion of your joints.
Balance
This is the ability to stand on your feet and avoid falls.
Speed
This refers to how quickly you move.

Being healthy and fit is linked with a lower risk of chronic diseases, as well as a better ability to manage health issues that do come up. Better fitness and being fit promotes more functionality and mobility throughout ones life.
